Supporting Local Artisans

At Krishna Churi Bhandar, we directly source our products from hardworking families in Fazilka, India. By choosing our bangles, you're not just buying beautiful jewelry - you're supporting traditional craftsmanship and helping sustain local economies.

Our Story

The Beginning

Krishna Churi Bhandar was founded in 1980 with a simple mission: to bring the exquisite craftsmanship of Fazilka's bangle makers to a wider audience while ensuring fair compensation for their artistry.

What started as a small family initiative has grown into a thriving business that supports over 50 artisan families in the Fazilka region.

Our Artisans

Fazilka has been the glass manufacturing capital of India for centuries. The families we work with have perfected their craft over generations, combining traditional techniques with contemporary designs.

Many of these artisans work in small home-based units, with entire families contributing to the bangle-making process - from glass blowing to lac application and intricate detailing.
